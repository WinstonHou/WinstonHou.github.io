Varsity Basketball

A leadership role centered on preparation, accountability, film review, and the development of younger players.

Role: Team Captain
The Assignment

What needed to be solved

My responsibility was to help the team improve its preparation and culture while keeping players accountable to shared standards.

My Role

What I contributed

  • Organized training support and mentored underclassmen.
  • Used game film to identify patterns, improve decision-making, and clarify team responsibilities.
  • Led through preparation and accountability during difficult stretches.
Reflection

What I learned

Basketball taught me that leadership is most visible when a team is under pressure. The captain’s job is to create clarity, maintain standards, and help others respond productively.
