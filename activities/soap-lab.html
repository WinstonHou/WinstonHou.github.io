High School Soap Lab

A student social enterprise that transforms waste cooking oil into sustainable soap and directs proceeds toward community causes.

Role: Head of Strategy
The Assignment

What needed to be solved

The team needed a strategy for turning an environmental project into a product people would understand, support, and purchase.

My Role

What I contributed

  • Led pricing, sales planning, fundraising strategy, and external positioning.
  • Helped shape partnerships, workshops, and the project’s community message.
  • Encouraged quieter teammates to pitch directly to customers and take ownership of the mission.
Reflection

What I learned

The project changed how I think about strategy. A plan becomes useful only when the team can communicate it, carry it out, and adapt it to real people.
