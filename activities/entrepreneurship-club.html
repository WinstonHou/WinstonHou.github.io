Model Entrepreneurship Club

A student-led program that teaches entrepreneurship through structured market analysis, product development, and pitching.

Role: Founder & President
The Assignment

What needed to be solved

I wanted to create a practical curriculum that moved students from vague business ideas toward clearer decisions and stronger execution.

My Role

What I contributed

  • Designed a 20-week curriculum covering markets, products, business models, and pitches.
  • Led weekly lessons and guided students through team-based projects.
  • Created a structure that made entrepreneurship more understandable and less abstract.
Reflection

What I learned

Teaching forced me to simplify complex ideas without making them shallow. I learned to lead by creating structure, asking better questions, and helping others make decisions independently.
